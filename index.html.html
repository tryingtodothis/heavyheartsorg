About

Mental health has a weight to it that most people don't talk about. HeavyHearts started because that weight became impossible to ignore. Because words thrown around carelessly still land somewhere. Because advocacy shouldn't wait until it's convenient.

Sometimes all it takes is one person who gets it. One shifted perspective can stop a joke before it leaves someone's mouth. Can make a friend pause. Can make the world, in some small way, less careless.
